It's easy to lose sight of the immensely original, colorful, unordered, and beautiful natural world that surrounds us. Especially when building machines, of which some are arguably artifact creators and dust merchants. But since that process is irreversible, we might as well ask: do those models come with their share of wonders, and can they help us to explore and shed light on unthought-of phenomena?
